## What is XtendOps?

XtendOps provides a powerful platform for creating **SmartAgents**: BPO-focused AI agents designed to handle customer interactions through automated workflows or assist human agents with AI-powered suggestions.

## Use Cases

### Automated Customer Service
Build fully automated SmartAgents that handle common customer inquiries end-to-end, reducing the need for human intervention.

### Agent Assist (Co-Pilot)
Empower your support team with AI-suggested responses that agents can review, edit, and send, improving efficiency while maintaining quality.

### Knowledge Augmentation
Leverage your knowledge base dynamically within conversations, ensuring accurate and consistent information delivery.

### Quality Assurance
Systematically review conversations, track performance metrics, and continuously improve SmartAgent quality.
